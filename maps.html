Day 1: Historical Peninsula

The T1 tram line connects all these attractions. Take the tram from Tophane station near the cruise port to Sultanahmet station.

Sultanahmet district is compact and walkable. Most attractions are within 5-10 minutes walking distance of each other.

Day 2: Beyoglu & Modern Istanbul

This area is easily accessible from the cruise port. Galata Tower is just a 10-15 minute walk, and Istiklal Street connects to Taksim Square.

There's a historic red tram that runs along Istiklal Street, but the area is best explored on foot.
